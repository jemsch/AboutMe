About

Hi, I'm Jemma — I'd like to call myself a knowledge junkie with a penchant for aesthetics.

I love to study math, philosophy, and politics, as well as topics in economics, history, evolutionary biology, and music theory.

Yeah, I'm pretty much a jack of all trades.

In my spare time, I sail on the Peninsula Youth Sailing Foundation's Club 420 team, play piano, and learn French.

Currently, I am a junior at Design Tech High School in Redwood City, California, where I am the Treasurer of the Leadership Club, Vice President of the Model United Nations Team, and Co-Captain of the Sailing Team.
